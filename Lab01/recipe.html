Cheddar Peppercorn Biscuits

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 cups cake flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1 tablespoon cracked pepper
  • 1/2 lbs shredded cheddar cheese
  • 2 cups buttermilk

Instructions

  1. Mix flours, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, salt, pepper, and cheddar
  2. Cut softened butter into the flour mix. Try not to overwork, leaving butter in small pieces.
  3. Stir in buttermilk carefully without overmixing just until mixture comes together.
  4. Flour surface, roll out, and cut biscuits.
  5. Bake at 375 on a lightly greased baking sheet for 15-18 minutes
