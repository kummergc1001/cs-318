About Me

Grace Kummerfeldt is a student at the University of Wisconsin - Eau Claire, Graduating spring of 2024. Grace is pursuing a major in Organizational Communication, a minor in Multimedia Communication, and a certificate in Arts Administration. Her career goal is to work in a fine arts organization such as a theater or music venue in the upper midwest area, supporting the local arts community.

Some highlights of Grace’s work experience has included helping organizing and working UWEC’s GeekCon 2022. Her role included contacting vendors and musicians who would be attending the convention to learn more about them and creating social media posts that showcase their talents. For the days leadings up to the event, Grace and other staff helped move in equipment for vendors and musicians. She played a role in helping inform the convention participants and attendees on the day of, doing tasks to help ensure the event goes smoothly. Grace has also briefly worked in the student organization, TV10, as a student reporter. Her job included contacting people involved in local stories, interviewing them, and editing together the content for the BNN (Blugold News Now) broadcast.

Some of Grace’s personal interests include listening and playing music. She plays flute, guitar, and loves to sing, participating in band and choir. In addition she loves plays and musicals, participating in several of them herself. Grace also loves movies, her favorite being Willy wonka and the Chocolate Factory (the Gene Wilder one of course)
